Description of the Service

The Service provides a way to connect your Google Classroom account to Claude Desktop through the Model Context Protocol (MCP). It consists of:

  • A web-based authorization flow that generates an OAuth refresh token
  • An MCP server (@salshah20/google-classroom-mcp) that runs locally on your computer and provides read-only access to your Google Classroom data
